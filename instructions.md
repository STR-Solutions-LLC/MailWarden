# MailWarden — User Guide

The previous terminal-based installation of MailWarden has been
deprecated. All setup, configuration, and day-to-day use now happens
through the installer `.pkg` and the built-in Dashboard.

**To install:** download the latest `.pkg` from the
[Releases page](https://github.com/STR-Solutions-LLC/MailWarden/releases)
and follow the steps in the [README](README.md).

**For documentation on features, training, and commands:** open
`/Applications/MailWarden.app` and click the **Help** tab in the
Dashboard. Everything is there, in plain English.

**For bugs, questions, or feedback:** info@rentalist.pro.
